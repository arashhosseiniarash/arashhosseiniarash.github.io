Introduction back to ToC

The HVAC FDD ontology is further extended by introducing classes required to accommodate knowledge and receive support from BIM and BAS/BMS to facilitate the AFDD process. The proposed ontology is extendable by means of adding further classes and/or relationships to allow answering/supporting other relevant competency questions/use cases. Facility managers, Asset managers, and building owners can benefit from adopting this ontology for HVAC maintenance.
